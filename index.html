Icaria

Objective: Move the Aeros (the object you control) to the other platform identical to the starting platform. You only complete the level once you are motionless and upright!

Controls

  • Main Thrust: Space
  • Reverse Thrust: Left Shift
  • Upper Left and Right (Tilt) Thrust: D, K
  • Lower Left and Right (Auxiliary) Thrust: C, M
  • Switch between normal and simple controls: B
    • Simple controls replaces tilt and auxiliary thrust with direct rotation.
  • Quit: Q

Advanced Tips

  • Hover: Firing both auxiliary thrusters at the same time will nearly counteract gravity.
  • Sideways Shift: Firing one tilt thruster and the opposite auxiliary thruster will shift the Aeros sideways while keeping it mostly upright.
  • Boost: Firing both the auxiliary thrusters can give an extra boost to the main thruster. Fire all five non-reverse thrusters for maximum boost.
  • Physics-based Control: If a tilt or auxiliary thruster is ever facing downwards, you can use it to generate a bit of lift.
